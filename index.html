Image Manipulator Tool
Full Stack / GUI

I worked on the "Image Manipulator & Enhancer" application at Northeastern University. This application was designed to be user-friendly and featured an intuitive interface, powered by the Swing framework. Its main purpose was to enable users to easily resize, crop, and enhance images, providing a seamless and engaging experience.

The application received positive feedback from more than 300 users, who appreciated its intuitive interface. As a result, user satisfaction increased by 40%, and the time spent on image editing tasks decreased by 30%. The positive reception and improved efficiency showcased the success of the project.






Alzheimer's Disease Detection
Full Stack / Mobile App

The "Alzheimer's Disease Detection using Mobile Game" project was a groundbreaking initiative aimed at detecting early signs of Alzheimer's disease through a mobile game. The game was carefully designed to challenge users' logical, cognitive, and memory retention abilities. By analyzing how players interacted with the game and performed in various tasks, valuable data on cognitive function and memory retention was collected.

To ensure the reliability of the results, the application was tested on a diverse group of over 300 users, ranging in age from 6 to 80 years. The data gathered from these tests was then meticulously analyzed to define optimum performance levels and identify cases that might indicate potential signs of Alzheimer's disease. Remarkably, the tests did not detect any indications of Alzheimer's disease in the participants, providing a sense of relief and reassurance.

The success of this project extended beyond the initial goals of detection. It also played a crucial role in raising awareness about Alzheimer's disease and promoting early detection in a non-invasive and engaging manner. The mobile game approach proved to be an innovative and effective method to monitor cognitive health and serve as a valuable tool for researchers and medical professionals. As a result, the project received widespread recognition and contributed significantly to the field of Alzheimer's disease research. Looking ahead, the data collected from this project holds promise for advancing future research and refining early detection methods for this devastating condition.




Hospital Management
Full Stack / Web App

Unity Health is a transformative project aimed at improving patients' access to healthcare services through a web-based appointment booking system. The current healthcare system faces challenges like long waiting times and limited options for patients, primarily relying on referrals. Unity Health seeks to change this by providing patients with a user-friendly platform to view detailed doctor profiles, including qualifications, specialties, and reviews from other patients. By eliminating biases and referral-based bookings, the system empowers patients to make informed decisions when selecting a doctor, without the need for prior connections.

The project's impact extends beyond local healthcare issues, as it has the potential to positively influence healthcare outcomes and patient satisfaction worldwide. Unity Health aims to streamline communication between doctors and patients by integrating with existing electronic health record systems, facilitating seamless appointment scheduling and rescheduling. Moreover, the centralized booking and scheduling system benefit hospitals by reducing administrative workload and improving operational efficiency. Through cutting-edge technology and data-driven insights, Unity Health strives to advance evidence-based decision-making in the healthcare sector. The project's commitment to addressing healthcare challenges reflects its purpose-driven approach to improving healthcare access and outcomes on a global scale.
